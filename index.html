CRYSTAL COLLECTOR

You will be given a random number at the beginning of the game.

There are four Crystals below, by clicking on a Crystal you will
add a specific amount of points to your Total.

You win the game by matching your Total to the Target number.
You lose the game if your Total goes above the Target number.

The value of each Crystal is hidden from you until you click on them.

Each time the game restarts, the value of the Target and your Crystals will change.

Good Luck!
